# Pine Script Indicators for Options Trading

Welcome to the Pine Script indicators repository tailored specifically for options trading and volatility analysis.

## Purpose

This repository is a collection of clean, maintainable, and reusable Pine Scripts designed for options traders. The primary goal is to help traders visualize volatility and mean reversion across both equities and cryptocurrency markets. 

By leveraging these indicators, traders can better identify overextended moves, potential reversions to the mean, and critical volatility bands to inform their options strategies (such as selling premium in high IV environments or buying premium in low IV environments).

## Contents

* `ia_mean_reversion.pine`: An InvestAnswers-inspired Mean Reversion strategy overlay, visualizing multiple standard deviation bands around a configurable moving average.
